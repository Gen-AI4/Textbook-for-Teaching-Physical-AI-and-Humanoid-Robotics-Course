# Introduction to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook is designed to provide a thorough understanding of the principles, technologies, and applications of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## What You'll Learn

In this textbook, you'll explore:

- The fundamentals of Physical AI and its relationship to traditional AI
- The architecture of humanoid robots and their unique challenges
- ROS 2 as the robotic nervous system for humanoid robots
- Simulation-based learning approaches for robot development
- Safety and ethical considerations in physical AI systems
- Practical applications of humanoid robots in various fields

## How to Use This Textbook

This textbook is structured with the following pedagogical features:

- Sequential modules that build upon each other
- Practical examples with ROS 2 code
- Simulation exercises with Gazebo
- Safety and ethical considerations integrated throughout
- Multilingual support for diverse learners

## Prerequisites

Before starting this textbook, we recommend:

- Basic programming knowledge (especially Python)
- Understanding of fundamental engineering concepts
- Familiarity with Linux command line (helpful but not required)
